Seminar Information

The Business and Marketing Analytics Seminar has the following objectives and structure:

Objectives

  • Promote research: Present papers, share comments and feedback, and develop new ideas.
  • Support PhD students: Provide an opportunity to discuss papers, offer guidance on their research and academic journey.
  • Build a community: Provide networking opportunities, foster research collaboration, and create a supportive environment.

Structure

  • Virtual (Zoom) Format
  • 60-Minute Talk: 45‑minute presentation, 15‑minute Q&A or discussion (with a PhD discussant).
  • (Optional) Informal conversation after each talk.
